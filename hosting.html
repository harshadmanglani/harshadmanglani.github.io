Hosting

Until the innovation of cloud services, if you wanted your own website, you had to buy/rent physical servers. This brought along not only server costs but also other costs such as renting physical space, cooling systems for servers, paying for on-location security, hiring someone who knows how to work with servers, etc.
Luckily, for us, long gone are the days of incurring all these costs just to host a website. Ever since Amazon came out with Amazon Web Services, more commonly known as AWS, and as other giants followed, we can now host a website in minutes! We'll be using Microsoft Azure as our Cloud Service provider and we'll be hosting our website as well as our database on Azure.

NOTE: You'll need a student account (you can get one by emailing Azure support with your college ID card) on Azure before you proceed so you can work with the $100 credit given to you instead of having to actually pay.
